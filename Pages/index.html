Topic Fact Details
Geography Florida has the longest coastline in the contiguous United States, at 1,350 miles. This extensive coastline includes beautiful beaches, barrier islands, and significant natural habitats.
Economy Tourism is a major industry in Florida, attracting over 130 million visitors annually. Major attractions like Walt Disney World, Universal Studios, and the Kennedy Space Center are key drivers of tourism.
Culture Florida is known for its diverse culture, heavily influenced by Latin American and Caribbean heritage. Cities like Miami are cultural melting pots, showcasing vibrant music, food, and festivals reflecting this diversity.
